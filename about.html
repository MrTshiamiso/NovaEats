Our Story

Founded in 2020, Nova Eats began as a small family dream to bring gourmet, globally-inspired cuisine to Botswana. Today, we are proud to be one of the leading modern restaurants in Gaborone.

Our name "Nova" means new star — reflecting our mission to shine brightly in every dish we serve.

Our Mission

To serve exceptional meals crafted from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, while providing a warm and welcoming experience for every guest.
